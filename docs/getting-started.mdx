# Getting Started with CultureAI

Welcome to CultureAI! This guide will help you get started with your cultural intelligence platform.

## What is CultureAI?

CultureAI is a cultural intelligence platform that helps brands understand their performance, analyze competitors, benchmark against industry standards, and create AI-powered content. It transforms video data into actionable insights and strategic recommendations.

## Navigation Overview

The CultureAI dashboard is organized into main sections accessible from the sidebar:

![CultureAI Dashboard Sidebar Navigation](/images/screenshots/navigation/sidebar.png)

Main navigation sidebar showing all sections

| Section | What It Does |
|--------|--------------|
| **Home** | Copilot AI assistant — your command center |
| **Brand Hub** | Your brand's cultural health and performance |
| **Competitive** | Leaderboard and competitor analysis |
| **Industry Lens** | Benchmark against cultural lanes |
| **Create** | AI content studio for video generation |
| **Reports** | Strategic reports and analysis documents |

## Brand Selection

At the top of the sidebar, you'll see a **Brand Selector**. This lets you switch between different brands in your portfolio (if you're managing multiple brands).

![Brand Selector Dropdown](/images/screenshots/navigation/brand-selector.png)

Use the brand selector to switch between brands in your portfolio

<Info>
Make sure you've selected the correct brand before diving into analysis. All data and insights are brand-specific.
</Info>

## Your First Steps

1. **Start with Copilot** — Ask "Show me my brand sources" to ensure your brand is set up correctly
2. **Explore Brand Hub** — Get familiar with your brand's performance metrics
3. **Check Competitive** — See how you stack up against competitors
4. **Try Content Studio** — Generate your first AI video idea
