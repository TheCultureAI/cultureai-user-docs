## Tips & Best Practices

### General Tips

**Start with Overview**
- Always check the Overview section first
- It provides executive-level insights
- Helps you understand the big picture

**Use Control Planes**
- Competitive and Industry have control planes
- Adjust time ranges, platforms, and metrics
- Normalize data when comparing different-sized brands

**Generate Fresh Reports**
- Reports are snapshots in time
- Generate new reports when you need updated insights
- Reports take 2-5 minutes to generate

**Explore Data Tabs**
- Data sections provide detailed analytics
- Use to understand specific dimensions
- Look for high-lift, low-saturation opportunities

### Brand Hub Tips

**Module Navigation**
- Each module provides focused insights
- Start with Executive Summary
- End with Action Plan for next steps

**Data Analysis**
- Formats tab shows what content types work
- Emotions tab reveals audience connections
- Narratives tab identifies story patterns
- Look for whitespace (high lift, low saturation)

**Opportunities**
- Whitespace tab highlights quick wins
- Playbook tab provides strategic moves
- Prioritize based on impact and effort

### Competitive Tips

**Use Normalization**
- Enable normalization when comparing brands
- Accounts for follower count differences
- Provides fair comparison

**Check Battlecards**
- Battlecards show competitor positioning
- Identify their strengths and weaknesses
- Find differentiation opportunities

**Winning Formulas**
- Review universal winning combinations
- Study competitor secret sauces
- Adapt what works for your brand

### Industry Lens Tips

**Time Windows**
- Use "Last 30 Days" for current trends
- Use "All Time" for stable benchmarks
- Adjust based on your needs

**Benchmark Heatmap**
- Overview tab shows benchmark heatmap
- Quick visual of format/emotion performance
- Identify high-performing territories

**Playbooks**
- Review easy/medium/hard plays
- Start with easy wins
- Plan for harder strategic moves

### Content Studio Tips

**Start with Ideas**
- Generate ideas first
- Ideas are informed by your data
- Select promising ones for creation

**Use Recipe Builder**
- Format selection is key
- Ingredients are performance-ranked
- Trust the auto-suggestions (they're data-backed)

**Iterate and Refine**
- Generate multiple variants
- Compare side-by-side
- Save winners as templates

**Director Controls**
- Fine-tune with Director Controls
- Experiment with camera presets
- Adjust motion intensity

**Template Library**
- Save successful recipes
- Reuse what works
- Build a library of proven patterns

### Reports Tips

**Generate When Needed**
- Reports are comprehensive but time-consuming
- Generate for important meetings
- Use Hub views for day-to-day analysis

**Review All Sections**
- Reports have multiple modules
- Each provides different insights
- Read the full narrative

**Use for Planning**
- Reports provide strategic recommendations
- Use action plans for roadmap
- Reference for decision-making

### Copilot Tips

**Be Specific**
- Specific questions get better answers
- Include context in your requests
- Use natural language

**Follow Up**
- Copilot remembers conversation
- Ask follow-up questions
- Build on previous responses

**Use Cards**
- Interactive cards provide quick actions
- Click buttons to take action
- Review before confirming changes

## Common Questions

### "How often should I check my Brand Hub?"

We recommend:
- **Weekly** — Check Overview for key metrics
- **Monthly** — Deep dive into Data sections
- **Quarterly** — Generate fresh Strategic Report

### "When should I generate reports?"

Generate reports when:
- Preparing for strategic meetings
- Quarterly business reviews
- Board presentations
- Major planning sessions

Reports take 2-5 minutes, so generate when you need comprehensive insights.

### "How do I know which formats to use?"

Check:
1. **Brand Hub → Data → Formats** — See what works for you
2. **Competitive → Compare → Benchmarking** — See what competitors use
3. **Industry Lens → Data → Formats** — See industry benchmarks
4. **Content Studio → Ideas** — Get AI suggestions

Look for high lift + low saturation = opportunity.

### "What's the difference between Hub and Report views?"

- **Hub** — Real-time dashboard with metrics and quick insights
- **Report** — Comprehensive narrative document for presentations

Use Hub for day-to-day analysis, Reports for strategic planning.

### "How do I create better videos?"

1. **Start with data** — Check what formats/emotions/narratives work
2. **Generate ideas** — Use AI-powered idea generator
3. **Use Recipe Builder** — Trust performance-ranked ingredients
4. **Iterate** — Generate variants and compare
5. **Save templates** — Reuse what works

### "Can I compare my brand to specific competitors?"

Yes! In Competitive:
1. Go to **Compare → Leaderboard**
2. See your rank vs all competitors
3. Go to **Compare → Benchmarking** for head-to-head
4. Check **Battlecards** for detailed competitor analysis

### "How do I find content opportunities?"

Check:
1. **Brand Hub → Opportunities → Whitespace** — High-lift, low-saturation
2. **Competitive → Opportunities → Whitespace** — Underexploited territories
3. **Industry Lens → Opportunities → Whitespace** — Industry opportunities
4. **Content Studio → Ideas** — AI-generated opportunities

### "What metrics should I focus on?"

Focus on:
- **Views Lift** — Performance vs baseline
- **Engagement Rate** — Quality of attention
- **Saturation** — How crowded a territory is
- **Whitespace Score** — Opportunity potential

High lift + low saturation = best opportunities.
