# Reports

The Reports screen provides access to three strategic reports that synthesize your brand, competitive, and industry data into comprehensive narrative documents.

![Reports Overview](/images/screenshots/reports/overview.png)

Reports screen showing available strategic reports

## Report Types

### 1. Brand Strategic Report

**What it is:** Comprehensive strategic analysis of your brand's performance and cultural resonance

**What it includes:**
- All 8 strategic modules in narrative form
- Executive summary
- Detailed analysis and recommendations
- Evidence and examples
- Strategic action plan

**When to use:**
- Brand performance reviews
- Strategic planning sessions
- Board presentations
- Quarterly business reviews

### 2. Competitive Strategic Report

**What it is:** Strategic competitive analysis with market positioning and actionable playbooks

**What it includes:**
- Executive summary with performance leaderboard
- Head-to-head benchmarking
- Cultural reference competitive analysis
- Winning formulas
- Strategic playbook for new entrants
- Top-performing video examples

**When to use:**
- Competitive strategy sessions
- Market positioning discussions
- Competitive advantage planning
- Stakeholder updates

### 3. Industry Strategic Report

**What it is:** Industry-wide strategic insights, benchmarks, and opportunities

**What it includes:**
- Industry performance context and benchmarks
- Format and creative strategy intelligence
- Emotional and narrative territory analysis
- Visual and aesthetic codes
- Cultural and metaphoric archetypes
- Discovery and demand signals
- Audience segments and subcultures
- Creative efficiency and production economics
- Strategic opportunity map and priority plays

**When to use:**
- Industry analysis
- Market research
- Strategic planning
- Trend identification

## Report Status

Reports can have different statuses:

- **Available** — Report is ready to view
- **Processing** — Report is being generated
- **Queued** — Report is waiting to be generated
- **Failed** — Generation encountered an error (tap to retry)

## Generating Reports

**How to generate:**
1. Navigate to the Reports screen
2. Find the report you want
3. If status is "Queued" or "Failed", click to generate
4. Wait for processing to complete
5. Click to view when status is "Available"

**Generation time:**
- Most reports generate in 2-5 minutes
- Complex reports may take longer
- You'll see progress updates

## Viewing Reports

![Viewing a Strategic Report](/images/screenshots/reports/viewing.png)

Strategic report view with table of contents and narrative sections

**Report structure:**
- **Header** — Brand name, generation date, key metrics
- **Table of Contents** — Navigate between sections
- **Modules/Sections** — Detailed analysis and insights
- **Evidence** — Examples and supporting data

**Navigation:**
- Use table of contents to jump to sections
- Scroll through narrative content
- Expand/collapse sections
- View embedded examples

**Exporting:**
- Reports can be exported or shared
- Use for presentations and planning
- Reference for strategic decisions

## Tips

<Warning>
Reports are comprehensive but time-consuming to generate. Generate them for important meetings, not for day-to-day analysis. Use Hub views for regular insights.
</Warning>

- Generate reports when preparing for strategic meetings
- Review all sections for complete insights
- Use reports for board presentations and planning
- Reference reports for decision-making
- Generate fresh reports when you need updated insights
