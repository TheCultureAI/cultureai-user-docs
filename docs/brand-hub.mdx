# Brand Hub

Brand Hub is your command center for understanding your brand's cultural performance. It provides deep insights into what's working, what's not, and where opportunities lie.

## Workspace Structure

Brand Hub is organized into four main sections:

1. **Overview** — Executive modules, key moves, and current narrative
2. **Data** — Dimension drill-down across formats, emotions, narratives, and hooks
3. **Opportunities** — Whitespace and playbook moves prioritized for action
4. **Strategy** — Full narrative report and strategic plan

## Overview Section

The Overview section contains **8 strategic modules** that provide executive-level insights:

### 1. Executive Summary

**What it shows:** Board-ready strategic tension and mandate

**Key insights:**
- Overall brand health score
- Top-performing content areas
- Critical gaps and opportunities
- Strategic recommendations

**What to look for:**
- Performance indicators (green = strong, amber = needs attention, red = critical)
- Key moves that drive results
- Evidence from your top videos

### 2. Brand Narrative & Creative Identity

**What it shows:** What your content says about who you are

**Key insights:**
- Dominant narrative patterns
- Creative identity strengths
- Brand voice consistency
- Storytelling effectiveness

### 3. Audience & Cultural Resonance

**What it shows:** Who resonates with your content and who doesn't

**Key insights:**
- Audience segments that engage most
- Emotional connections that work
- Cultural references that land
- Missed opportunities

### 4. Content Portfolio & Format Strategy

**What it shows:** Which formats pull their weight

**Key insights:**
- Best-performing content formats
- Format efficiency (ROI)
- Format saturation levels
- Whitespace opportunities

<Info>
Look for formats with high views lift and low saturation — these are your best opportunities.
</Info>

### 5. Messaging & Value Proposition

**What it shows:** What you're saying vs what lands

**Key insights:**
- Message clarity and consistency
- Value proposition effectiveness
- Hook patterns that work
- Messaging gaps

### 6. Platform Optimization

**What it shows:** Platform-specific performance and recommendations

**Key insights:**
- TikTok vs Instagram performance
- Platform-specific best practices
- Cross-platform opportunities
- Platform elasticity

### 7. Creative Efficiency & Operations

**What it shows:** Production ROI and team implications

**Key insights:**
- Content production efficiency
- Creative saturation levels
- Production cost vs performance
- Recommended cadence

### 8. Strategic Action Plan

**What it shows:** Prioritized 0-180 day roadmap

**Key insights:**
- Immediate actions (0-30 days)
- Medium-term plays (30-90 days)
- Long-term strategy (90-180 days)
- Guardrails to avoid

## Data Section

The Data section provides detailed analytics across multiple dimensions. Use the tabs to explore:

### Formats Tab

**What it shows:** Performance of different content formats

**Metrics:**
- **Views Lift** — How your format performs vs industry baseline
- **Count** — How many videos use this format
- **Saturation** — How crowded this format is in your industry

**What to look for:**
- Formats with high lift and low saturation (opportunities)
- Formats you're overusing (saturation risk)
- Formats competitors are winning with

### Emotions Tab

**What it shows:** Emotional performance landscape

**Metrics:**
- Primary emotions that drive engagement
- Secondary emotional layers
- Emotional intensity levels

### Narratives Tab

**What it shows:** Narrative arc performance

**Metrics:**
- Narrative structures that perform best
- Story arc effectiveness
- Narrative complexity vs engagement

### Aesthetics Tab

**What it shows:** Visual style performance

**Metrics:**
- Aesthetic archetypes that work
- Visual style effectiveness
- Aesthetic saturation

### Hooks Tab

**What it shows:** Hook pattern performance

**Metrics:**
- Hook types that grab attention
- Hook latency (when hook appears)
- Hook effectiveness

### Combinations Tab

**What it shows:** Winning format + ingredient combinations

**Metrics:**
- Top-performing combinations
- Combination lift scores
- Combination frequency

## Opportunities Section

The Opportunities section highlights actionable moves:

### Whitespace Tab

**What it shows:** High-lift, low-saturation opportunities

**What to look for:**
- Formats/emotions/narratives with high potential
- Underexploited content territories
- Quick wins you can implement

### Playbook Tab

**What it shows:** Strategic playbook moves

**What to look for:**
- Prioritized actions based on data
- Plays that align with brand strategy
- Moves that competitors aren't making

## Strategy Section

The Strategy section contains the full **Brand Strategic Report** — a comprehensive narrative document perfect for board presentations and strategic planning.

**What it includes:**
- All 8 strategic modules in narrative form
- Detailed analysis and recommendations
- Evidence and examples
- Long-term strategic vision

**How to use it:**
- Generate a fresh report when you need updated insights
- Export or share for presentations
- Use as a reference for strategic planning

## Tips

<Warning>
Always check the Overview section first — it provides executive-level insights that help you understand the big picture.
</Warning>

- Use the Data section to understand specific dimensions
- Look for whitespace (high lift, low saturation) in Opportunities
- Generate fresh Strategic Reports for important meetings
- Review all 8 modules to get a complete picture
